## ðŸ“… Tasks & Meetings
Stay organized and ensure no follow-up is missed.

### How to Use
1.  **Setting Tasks**: Create tasks like "Send Proposal" or "Demo Call" with specific due dates and times.
2.  **Scheduled Meetings**: Use the "Meetings" module to book appointments. Integrated Google Meet links are generated automatically.
3.  **Daily Targets**: Check your dashboard to see your pending tasks for the day to meet your `Daily Target`.

### Scenarios & Examples
> [!WARNING]
> **Scenario: Handling Overdue Tasks**
> You missed a follow-up call yesterday.
> - **Action**: The task card will turn red and pulse with an "Overdue" badge.
> - **Fix**: Click "Reschedule" to move it to today or tomorrow to keep the pipeline moving.
